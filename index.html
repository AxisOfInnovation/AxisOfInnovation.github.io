The Axis of Innovation

We are an FTC team, and aspiring FRC team should we get the funds to do so, out of Colleyville, Texas, since 2013 (although many of our members are a splinter group from another, now defunct, FTC team in 2012). We have made it to the Super-Regional, the third of four levels in FTC, every year since we were founded.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to offer middle and high school students an opportunity to work with robotics technology hands-on, allowing them to learn how to succeed in the world of engineering, in- and outside of the classroom. By focusing on experience through robotics competitions with other high and middle school students on other teams, we spur competition and cooperation in the design and production processes, as they would experience in the real world.
